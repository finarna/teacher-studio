# JEE Main 2026 Official Syllabus

**Official Source**: National Testing Agency (NTA)
**Release Date**: January 2026
**Website**: https://jeemain.nta.ac.in

## Exam Pattern

- **Total Questions**: 90 (30 per subject)
- **Duration**: 180 minutes (3 hours)
- **Subjects**: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
- **Marking**: +4 for correct, -1 for incorrect
- **Class Coverage**: Class 11 (40%) + Class 12 (60%)

---

## Mathematics - Class 12 (13 Chapters)

### Unit 1: Relations and Functions
1. **Relations and Functions**
   - Types of relations, Functions
   - Composition, Invertibility
   - **Weightage**: 4-5 marks

2. **Inverse Trigonometric Functions**
   - Domain, Range, Properties
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

### Unit 2: Algebra
3. **Matrices**
   - Types, Operations, Properties
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

4. **Determinants**
   - Properties, Adjoint, Inverse
   - Applications (Cramer's rule)
   - **Weightage**: 6 marks

### Unit 3: Calculus
5. **Continuity and Differentiability**
   - Limits, Continuity, Derivatives
   - Chain rule, Parametric forms
   - **Weightage**: 8 marks (HIGH PRIORITY)

6. **Applications of Derivatives**
   - Rate of change, Tangents/Normals
   - Maxima/Minima, Approximations
   - **Weightage**: 6 marks

7. **Integrals**
   - Integration techniques, Definite integrals
   - Fundamental theorem
   - **Weightage**: 8 marks (HIGH PRIORITY)

8. **Applications of Integrals**
   - Area under curves, Volume
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

9. **Differential Equations**
   - Formation, Order, Degree
   - Solutions (variable separable, linear)
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

### Unit 4: Vectors and 3D Geometry
10. **Vectors**
    - Scalar/Vector products
    - Scalar triple product
    - **Weightage**: 6 marks

11. **Three Dimensional Geometry**
    - Direction cosines, Lines, Planes
    - Angles, Distance
    - **Weightage**: 6 marks

### Unit 5: Statistics and Probability
12. **Linear Programming**
    - Mathematical formulation, Graphical method
    - **Weightage**: 4 marks

13. **Probability**
    - Conditional probability, Bayes' theorem
    - Random variables, Binomial distribution
    - **Weightage**: 6 marks

## Physics - Class 12 (14 Chapters)

### Unit 1: Electrostatics
1. **Electric Charges and Fields**
   - Coulomb's Law, Electric Field
   - Gauss's Law
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

2. **Electrostatic Potential and Capacitance**
   - Potential, Capacitors, Energy
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

### Unit 2: Current Electricity
3. **Current Electricity**
   - Ohm's Law, Resistance combinations
   - Kirchhoff's laws, Wheatstone bridge
   - **Weightage**: 5 marks

### Unit 3: Magnetic Effects
4. **Moving Charges and Magnetism**
   - Biot-Savart, Ampere's law
   - Force on current-carrying conductor
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

5. **Magnetism and Matter**
   - Magnetic dipole, Earth's magnetism
   - **Weightage**: 3 marks

### Unit 4: Electromagnetic Induction
6. **Electromagnetic Induction**
   - Faraday's law, Lenz's law
   - Self/Mutual induction
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

7. **Alternating Current**
   - AC circuits, LCR resonance
   - Power, Transformers
   - **Weightage**: 4 marks

### Unit 5: Electromagnetic Waves
8. **Electromagnetic Waves**
   - Characteristics, EM spectrum
   - **Weightage**: 2 marks

### Unit 6: Optics
9. **Ray Optics and Optical Instruments**
   - Reflection, Refraction, Lenses
   - Optical instruments
   - **Weightage**: 5 marks

10. **Wave Optics**
    - Interference, Diffraction, Polarization
    - **Weightage**: 3 marks

### Unit 7: Modern Physics
11. **Dual Nature of Radiation and Matter**
    - Photoelectric effect, Matter waves
    - **Weightage**: 3 marks

12. **Atoms**
    - Bohr's model, Energy levels
    - **Weightage**: 2 marks

13. **Nuclei**
    - Radioactivity, Nuclear reactions
    - **Weightage**: 3 marks

14. **Semiconductor Electronics**
    - Diode, Transistor, Logic gates
    - **Weightage**: 4 marks

## Preparation Strategy

### High-Weightage Topics (Focus Areas)

**Mathematics:**
- Continuity and Differentiability (8 marks)
- Integrals (8 marks)
- Determinants (6 marks)
- Vectors (6 marks)
- 3D Geometry (6 marks)

**Physics:**
- Current Electricity (5 marks)
- Ray Optics (5 marks)
- Electrostatics (4+4 = 8 marks)
- AC Circuits (4 marks)

**Chemistry:**
- Same high-weightage topics as NEET

### Study Hours Allocation

- **Total Study Hours**: 1500 hours (over 12 months)
- **Mathematics**: 600 hours (40%)
- **Physics**: 500 hours (33%)
- **Chemistry**: 400 hours (27%)

### Key Differences from NEET

1. **More Math-Heavy**: 40% of paper is Mathematics
2. **Higher Difficulty**: Conceptual depth required
3. **Speed Matters**: 90 questions in 180 minutes (2 min/question)
4. **No Biology**: Focus on PCM only
