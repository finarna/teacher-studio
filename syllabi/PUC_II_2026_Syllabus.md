# Karnataka PUC II (2nd PUC) Syllabus 2025-26

**Official Source**: Department of Pre-University Education (DPUE), Karnataka
**Assessment Board**: Karnataka School Examination and Assessment Board (KSEAB)
**Official Website**: https://pue.karnataka.gov.in / https://kseab.karnataka.gov.in
**Class Equivalent**: Class 12 / Higher Secondary (Second Year)

## Board Exam Pattern

- **Total Marks**: 100 per subject (70 theory + 30 practical)
- **Duration**: 3 hours 15 minutes
- **Passing Marks**: 35%
- **Subjects**: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
- **Syllabus Basis**: NCERT + Karnataka State additions

---

## Physics - 2nd PUC (14 Chapters)

**Theory**: 70 marks | **Practical**: 30 marks

### Part I (8 Chapters)

1. **Electric Charges and Fields**
   - Coulomb's Law, Electric Field
   - Electric Flux, Gauss's Law
   - Electric Dipole
   - **Marks**: 5-6

2. **Electrostatic Potential and Capacitance**
   - Electric Potential, Potential Energy
   - Capacitors, Dielectrics
   - Energy stored in capacitors
   - **Marks**: 5-6

3. **Current Electricity**
   - Electric Current, Ohm's Law
   - Resistance, Resistivity, Conductivity
   - Kirchhoff's Laws, Wheatstone Bridge
   - Cells (EMF, Internal resistance)
   - **Marks**: 7-8

4. **Moving Charges and Magnetism**
   - Magnetic Force on moving charge
   - Biot-Savart Law, Ampere's Law
   - Force on current-carrying conductor
   - Torque on current loop, Galvanometer
   - **Marks**: 5-6

5. **Magnetism and Matter**
   - Bar magnet as magnetic dipole
   - Magnetic field lines, Earth's magnetism
   - Magnetic properties of materials
   - **Marks**: 4-5

6. **Electromagnetic Induction**
   - Faraday's Law, Lenz's Law
   - Motional EMF
   - Self-induction, Mutual induction
   - Eddy currents, AC Generator
   - **Marks**: 6-7

7. **Alternating Current**
   - AC voltage and current
   - LCR circuits, Resonance
   - Power in AC circuits
   - Transformers
   - **Marks**: 5-6

8. **Electromagnetic Waves**
   - Displacement current
   - Electromagnetic spectrum
   - Characteristics of EM waves
   - **Marks**: 3-4

### Part II (6 Chapters)

9. **Ray Optics and Optical Instruments**
   - Reflection, Refraction
   - Total Internal Reflection
   - Lenses, Mirrors, Prisms
   - Optical instruments (Microscope, Telescope)
   - **Marks**: 7-8

10. **Wave Optics**
    - Huygens' Principle
    - Interference (Young's Double Slit Experiment)
    - Diffraction, Polarization
    - **Marks**: 5-6

11. **Dual Nature of Radiation and Matter**
    - Photoelectric effect, Einstein's photoelectric equation
    - Matter waves, de Broglie wavelength
    - Davisson-Germer experiment
    - **Marks**: 4-5

12. **Atoms**
    - Alpha particle scattering
    - Bohr's model of hydrogen atom
    - Energy levels, Line spectra
    - **Marks**: 4-5

13. **Nuclei**
    - Composition, Size, Mass-energy relation
    - Radioactivity, Nuclear reactions
    - Binding energy
    - **Marks**: 5-6

14. **Semiconductor Electronics: Materials, Devices and Simple Circuits**
    - Energy bands, Intrinsic/Extrinsic semiconductors
    - p-n junction, Diode characteristics
    - Transistor, Amplifier
    - Logic gates
    - **Marks**: 5-6

### Practicals (30 marks)
- Experiments on optics, electricity, magnetism
- Viva voce
- Record + Journal

## Chemistry - 2nd PUC (Modified - Some Chapters Deleted)

**Theory**: 70 marks | **Practical**: 30 marks

### Included Chapters (13 total)

1. **Solutions**
   - Types of solutions, Concentration terms
   - Raoult's Law, Ideal/Non-ideal solutions
   - Colligative properties
   - **Marks**: 6-7

2. **Electrochemistry**
   - Electrochemical cells, Electrode potential
   - Nernst equation, Conductance
   - Batteries, Fuel cells, Corrosion
   - **Marks**: 7-8 (Highest weightage)

3. **Chemical Kinetics**
   - Rate of reaction, Factors affecting rate
   - Order, Molecularity
   - Rate law, Integrated rate equations
   - Arrhenius equation, Catalysis
   - **Marks**: 6-7

4. **Surface Chemistry** (MAY BE DELETED - Check official PDF)
   - Adsorption, Catalysis
   - Colloids, Emulsions
   - **Marks**: 4-5

5. **General Principles and Processes of Isolation of Elements**
   - Occurrence, Concentration
   - Extraction methods
   - Refining, Uses
   - **Marks**: 4-5

6. **p-Block Elements**
   - Group 15 elements (Nitrogen family)
   - Group 16 elements (Oxygen family)
   - Group 17 elements (Halogens)
   - Group 18 elements (Noble gases)
   - **Marks**: 6-7

7. **d and f Block Elements**
   - Transition elements, Properties
   - Lanthanides, Actinides
   - **Marks**: 5-6

8. **Coordination Compounds**
   - Nomenclature, Isomerism
   - Bonding theories (VBT, CFT)
   - Applications
   - **Marks**: 6-7

9. **Haloalkanes and Haloarenes**
   - Nomenclature, Preparation
   - Physical/Chemical properties
   - Reaction mechanisms (SN1, SN2, Elimination)
   - **Marks**: 5-6

10. **Alcohols, Phenols and Ethers**
    - Nomenclature, Preparation
    - Physical/Chemical properties
    - Distinction tests
    - **Marks**: 5-6

11. **Aldehydes, Ketones and Carboxylic Acids**
    - Nomenclature, Preparation
    - Chemical reactions, Mechanisms
    - Uses
    - **Marks**: 7-8 (Highest weightage)

12. **Amines**
    - Classification, Nomenclature
    - Preparation, Properties
    - Diazonium salts
    - **Marks**: 4-5

13. **Biomolecules**
    - Carbohydrates, Proteins
    - Vitamins, Nucleic acids
    - Enzymes, Hormones
    - **Marks**: 5-6

### Deleted Chapters (Confirmed for 2025-26)
- ❌ **Chapter 1**: Solid State
- ❌ **Chapter 5**: Surface Chemistry (possibly)
- ❌ **Chapter 6**: General Principles of Isolation
- ❌ **Chapter 7**: (Check official PDF)
- ❌ **Chapter 15**: Polymers
- ❌ **Chapter 16**: Chemistry in Everyday Life

### Practicals (30 marks)
- Salt analysis, Titrations
- Organic compound preparation
- Viva voce, Record

## Biology - 2nd PUC (13 Chapters)

**Theory**: 70 marks | **Practical**: 30 marks

### Botany (8 Chapters)

1. **Sexual Reproduction in Flowering Plants**
   - Flower structure, Pre-fertilization events
   - Pollination, Fertilization
   - Post-fertilization events, Seed formation
   - **Marks**: 6-7

2. **Principles of Inheritance and Variation**
   - Mendel's laws, Deviations
   - Chromosomal theory of inheritance
   - Linkage, Crossing over
   - Sex determination
   - **Marks**: 6-7

3. **Molecular Basis of Inheritance**
   - DNA structure, Replication
   - Transcription, Genetic code
   - Translation, Gene regulation
   - Human genome project
   - **Marks**: 7-8 (Highest weightage)

4. **Biotechnology: Principles and Processes**
   - Principles, Tools (Vectors, Enzymes)
   - Processes (rDNA technology, PCR, Cloning)
   - **Marks**: 5-6

5. **Biotechnology and its Applications**
   - Applications in agriculture, medicine
   - Transgenic organisms
   - Ethical issues, Biosafety
   - **Marks**: 5-6

6. **Organisms and Populations**
   - Organism and environment
   - Population attributes, Growth
   - Population interactions
   - **Marks**: 5-6

7. **Ecosystem**
   - Ecosystem structure, Function
   - Energy flow, Food chains
   - Nutrient cycling (Carbon, Nitrogen)
   - Ecological succession
   - **Marks**: 5-6

8. **Biodiversity and Conservation**
   - Biodiversity levels, Patterns
   - Loss of biodiversity
   - Conservation strategies
   - **Marks**: 5-6

### Zoology (5 Chapters)

9. **Human Reproduction**
   - Male and female reproductive systems
   - Gametogenesis, Menstrual cycle
   - Fertilization, Pregnancy, Parturition
   - Lactation
   - **Marks**: 7-8 (Highest weightage)

10. **Reproductive Health**
    - Need for reproductive health
    - Birth control methods, Medical termination
    - STDs, Infertility, ART
    - **Marks**: 4-5

11. **Human Health and Disease**
    - Common diseases (Bacterial, Viral, Protozoal)
    - Immunity (Innate, Acquired)
    - Vaccines, Cancer, AIDS
    - Drug and alcohol abuse
    - **Marks**: 6-7

12. **Microbes in Human Welfare**
    - Microbes in household products
    - Industrial products, Sewage treatment
    - Biogas production
    - **Marks**: 4-5

13. **Evolution**
    - Origin of life, Biological evolution
    - Evidence, Mechanisms (Natural selection)
    - Hardy-Weinberg principle
    - Human evolution
    - **Marks**: 5-6

### Practicals (30 marks)
- Experiments, Spotting
- Project work
- Viva voce, Record

## Mathematics - 2nd PUC (13 Chapters)

**Theory**: 80 marks | **Internal Assessment**: 20 marks

1. **Relations and Functions**
   - Types of relations, Functions
   - Composition, Invertibility
   - Binary operations
   - **Marks**: 5-6

2. **Inverse Trigonometric Functions**
   - Basic concepts, Domain, Range
   - Properties, Principal values
   - **Marks**: 4-5

3. **Matrices**
   - Types, Operations
   - Transpose, Symmetric/Skew symmetric
   - **Marks**: 5-6

4. **Determinants**
   - Properties, Expansion
   - Minors, Cofactors
   - Adjoint, Inverse
   - Applications (Solving linear equations)
   - **Marks**: 6-7

5. **Continuity and Differentiability**
   - Continuity, Differentiability
   - Chain rule, Parametric forms
   - Second order derivatives
   - Mean Value Theorem
   - **Marks**: 7-8

6. **Applications of Derivatives**
   - Rate of change, Tangents and normals
   - Increasing/Decreasing functions
   - Maxima and minima
   - Approximations
   - **Marks**: 6-7

7. **Integrals**
   - Integration as anti-derivative
   - Methods (Substitution, Parts, Partial fractions)
   - Definite integrals, Properties
   - Fundamental theorem of calculus
   - **Marks**: 9-10 (Highest weightage)

8. **Applications of Integrals**
   - Area under curves
   - Area between curves
   - **Marks**: 5-6

9. **Differential Equations**
   - Basic concepts, Formation
   - Order, Degree
   - Solutions (Variable separable, Linear, Bernoulli)
   - **Marks**: 5-6

10. **Vectors**
    - Vectors and scalars, Position vector
    - Addition, Multiplication by scalar
    - Scalar (dot) product, Vector (cross) product
    - Scalar triple product
    - **Marks**: 6-7

11. **Three Dimensional Geometry**
    - Direction cosines and ratios
    - Equation of line, Plane
    - Angle between lines, planes
    - Distance formulas
    - **Marks**: 6-7

12. **Linear Programming**
    - Mathematical formulation
    - Graphical method
    - Feasible region, Optimal solution
    - **Marks**: 4-5

13. **Probability**
    - Conditional probability
    - Multiplication theorem, Independent events
    - Bayes' theorem
    - Random variables, Probability distribution
    - Binomial distribution
    - **Marks**: 5-6

## Preparation Strategy

### High-Weightage Units (Focus Priority)

**Physics:**
- Current Electricity (7-8 marks)
- Ray Optics (7-8 marks)
- Electromagnetic Induction (6-7 marks)
- Electrostatics (5-6 marks each chapter)

**Chemistry:**
- Electrochemistry (7-8 marks)
- Aldehydes, Ketones, Carboxylic Acids (7-8 marks)
- Coordination Compounds (6-7 marks)
- Solutions (6-7 marks)

**Mathematics:**
- Integrals (9-10 marks)
- Continuity and Differentiability (7-8 marks)
- Determinants (6-7 marks)
- Vectors (6-7 marks)
- 3D Geometry (6-7 marks)

**Biology:**
- Molecular Basis of Inheritance (7-8 marks)
- Human Reproduction (7-8 marks)
- Sexual Reproduction in Plants (6-7 marks)
- Human Health and Disease (6-7 marks)
- Principles of Inheritance (6-7 marks)

### Study Plan

- **Total Study Hours**: 800 hours (Academic year)
- **Theory**: 600 hours
- **Practicals**: 100 hours
- **Revision**: 100 hours

### Board Exam Tips

1. **Follow Karnataka Textbooks**: KSEAB question paper closely follows state textbooks
2. **Previous Year Papers**: Practice 10 years of Karnataka PUC II papers
3. **NCERT is Base**: But Karnataka books have additional content
4. **Practical Record**: Maintain neat records for 30 marks
5. **Deleted Topics**: Don't waste time on deleted chapters
6. **Board Specific**: Some questions are Karnataka-specific, not just NCERT
