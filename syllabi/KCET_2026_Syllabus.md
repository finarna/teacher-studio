# KCET 2026 Official Syllabus (Karnataka CET)

**Official Source**: Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA)
**Release Date**: January 29, 2026
**Official Website**: https://cetonline.karnataka.gov.in

## Exam Pattern

- **Total Questions**: 180 (60 per subject)
- **Duration**: 240 minutes (4 hours total - 80 min per subject)
- **Subjects**: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology
- **Marking**: 1 mark per question, **NO negative marking**
- **Class Coverage**: 1st PUC (20%) + 2nd PUC (80%)

### Test Structure
- **Physics**: 60 questions, 80 minutes
- **Chemistry**: 60 questions, 80 minutes
- **Mathematics**: 60 questions, 80 minutes
- **Biology**: 60 questions, 80 minutes (for medical stream)

---

## Physics - Class 12 (14 Chapters)

**Syllabus Basis**: Karnataka 2nd PUC Physics (identical to NCERT Class 12)

### Part I (8 Chapters)

1. **Electric Charges and Fields**
   - Coulomb's Law, Electric Field, Electric Flux
   - Gauss's Law and applications
   - Electric Dipole
   - **Weightage**: High (80% from Class 12)

2. **Electrostatic Potential and Capacitance**
   - Electric Potential, Potential Energy
   - Capacitors, Dielectrics, Energy Storage
   - Combination of capacitors
   - **Weightage**: High

3. **Current Electricity**
   - Ohm's Law, Drift velocity
   - Kirchhoff's Laws, Wheatstone Bridge
   - Cells, Internal resistance
   - **Weightage**: Very High (Important chapter)

4. **Moving Charges and Magnetism**
   - Biot-Savart Law, Ampere's Law
   - Force on current-carrying conductor
   - Torque on current loop, Galvanometer
   - **Weightage**: High

5. **Magnetism and Matter**
   - Bar magnet, Magnetic field lines
   - Earth's magnetism
   - Magnetic properties of materials
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

6. **Electromagnetic Induction**
   - Faraday's Law, Lenz's Law
   - Motional EMF, Self and Mutual induction
   - Eddy currents, AC generator
   - **Weightage**: Very High (Important chapter)

7. **Alternating Current**
   - AC voltage and current
   - LCR circuits, Resonance
   - Power in AC circuits, Transformers
   - **Weightage**: High

8. **Electromagnetic Waves**
   - Displacement current
   - EM spectrum and characteristics
   - **Weightage**: Low

### Part II (6 Chapters)

9. **Ray Optics and Optical Instruments**
   - Reflection, Refraction, Total Internal Reflection
   - Lenses, Mirrors, Prisms
   - Optical instruments (Microscope, Telescope)
   - **Weightage**: Very High (Important chapter)

10. **Wave Optics**
    - Huygens' Principle
    - Interference (Young's Double Slit)
    - Diffraction, Polarization
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

11. **Dual Nature of Radiation and Matter**
    - Photoelectric effect, Einstein's equation
    - Matter waves, de Broglie wavelength
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

12. **Atoms**
    - Alpha scattering, Bohr's model
    - Energy levels, Hydrogen spectrum
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

13. **Nuclei**
    - Nucleus composition, Size
    - Radioactivity, Nuclear reactions
    - Binding energy, Mass-energy equivalence
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

14. **Semiconductor Electronics (and Communication Systems)**
    - p-n junction, Diode characteristics
    - Transistor, Amplifier, Logic gates
    - **Weightage**: High

### New Addition 2026
- **Experimental Skills Unit**: Practical-based questions on measurement, error analysis

## Chemistry - Class 12 (Modified Syllabus)

**Syllabus Basis**: Karnataka 2nd PUC Chemistry (some chapters deleted)

### Included Chapters

1. **Solutions**
   - Concentration terms, Raoult's Law
   - Colligative properties
   - **Weightage**: High

2. **Electrochemistry**
   - Electrochemical cells, Nernst equation
   - Conductance, Batteries, Corrosion
   - **Weightage**: Very High (14 marks - Maximum weightage)

3. **Chemical Kinetics**
   - Rate of reaction, Order, Molecularity
   - Arrhenius equation, Catalysis
   - **Weightage**: High

4. **The d and f Block Elements**
   - Properties, Oxidation states
   - Compounds, Uses
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

5. **Coordination Compounds**
   - Nomenclature, Bonding theories
   - Isomerism, Crystal Field Theory
   - **Weightage**: High

6. **Haloalkanes and Haloarenes**
   - Nomenclature, Preparation, Reactions
   - Mechanisms (SN1, SN2, Elimination)
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

7. **Alcohols, Phenols and Ethers**
   - Preparation, Properties, Reactions
   - Distinction tests
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

8. **Aldehydes, Ketones and Carboxylic Acids**
   - Preparation, Properties, Reactions
   - Mechanisms, Derivatives
   - **Weightage**: Very High (14 marks - Maximum weightage)

9. **Amines**
   - Classification, Preparation
   - Properties, Diazonium salts
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

10. **Biomolecules**
    - Carbohydrates, Proteins, Vitamins
    - Nucleic acids, Hormones
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

### Deleted Chapters (Not in KCET 2026)
- ❌ Chapter 1: Solid State
- ❌ Chapter 5: (Specific chapter - check official PDF)
- ❌ Chapter 6: (Specific chapter - check official PDF)
- ❌ Chapter 7: (Specific chapter - check official PDF)
- ❌ Chapter 15: Polymers
- ❌ Chapter 16: Chemistry in Everyday Life

### New Addition 2026
- **Principles Related to Practical Chemistry**: Experimental questions on chemical analysis

## Biology - Class 12 (13 Chapters)

**Syllabus Basis**: Karnataka 2nd PUC Biology (follows NCERT)

### Botany (6 Chapters)

1. **Sexual Reproduction in Flowering Plants**
   - Flower structure, Pollination types
   - Fertilization, Seed and fruit formation
   - **Weightage**: Very High

2. **Principles of Inheritance and Variation**
   - Mendel's laws, Chromosomal theory
   - Linkage, Crossing over
   - **Weightage**: Very High

3. **Molecular Basis of Inheritance**
   - DNA structure, Replication
   - Transcription, Translation, Genetic code
   - **Weightage**: Very High (Maximum weightage)

4. **Biotechnology: Principles and Processes**
   - Genetic engineering, PCR, Cloning
   - **Weightage**: High

5. **Biotechnology and its Applications**
   - GM crops, Gene therapy, Biosafety
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

6. **Organisms and Populations**
   - Population interactions, Adaptations
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

7. **Ecosystem**
   - Energy flow, Food chains, Nutrient cycling
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

8. **Biodiversity and Conservation**
   - Levels, Threats, Conservation strategies
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

### Zoology (5 Chapters)

9. **Human Reproduction**
   - Reproductive systems, Gametogenesis
   - Menstrual cycle, Pregnancy
   - **Weightage**: Very High (Maximum weightage)

10. **Reproductive Health**
    - Birth control methods, STDs
    - Infertility, ART
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

11. **Human Health and Disease**
    - Immunity, Vaccines
    - Cancer, AIDS, Drug abuse
    - **Weightage**: Very High

12. **Microbes in Human Welfare**
    - Microbes in household, industry
    - Sewage treatment, Biogas
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

13. **Evolution**
    - Origin of life, Evidence
    - Natural selection, Human evolution
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

### New Addition 2026
- **Principles Related to Experimental Biology**: Practical-based questions

## Mathematics - Class 12 (13 Chapters)

**Syllabus Basis**: Karnataka 2nd PUC Mathematics (follows NCERT)

1. **Relations and Functions**
   - Types of relations, Functions, Composition
   - Invertibility, Binary operations
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

2. **Inverse Trigonometric Functions**
   - Domain, Range, Properties
   - Principal values
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

3. **Matrices**
   - Types, Operations, Properties
   - Transpose, Symmetric matrices
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

4. **Determinants**
   - Properties, Expansion, Minors, Cofactors
   - Adjoint, Inverse, Applications
   - **Weightage**: High

5. **Continuity and Differentiability**
   - Limits, Continuity
   - Derivatives, Chain rule, Parametric forms
   - **Weightage**: High

6. **Applications of Derivatives**
   - Rate of change, Tangents/Normals
   - Increasing/Decreasing, Maxima/Minima
   - **Weightage**: High

7. **Integrals**
   - Integration methods, Definite integrals
   - Fundamental theorem, Properties
   - **Weightage**: Very High (20 marks - Maximum weightage)

8. **Applications of Integrals**
   - Area under curves, Area between curves
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

9. **Differential Equations**
   - Formation, Order, Degree
   - Solutions (variable separable, linear, Bernoulli)
   - **Weightage**: Moderate

10. **Vectors**
    - Position vector, Addition, Scalar/Vector products
    - Scalar triple product
    - **Weightage**: High

11. **Three Dimensional Geometry**
    - Direction cosines/ratios, Lines, Planes
    - Angle, Distance
    - **Weightage**: High

12. **Linear Programming**
    - Mathematical formulation, Graphical solution
    - Feasible region, Optimization
    - **Weightage**: Low

13. **Probability**
    - Conditional probability, Multiplication theorem
    - Bayes' theorem, Random variables, Binomial distribution
    - **Weightage**: Moderate

## Preparation Strategy

### High-Weightage Chapters (Focus Priority)

**Physics:**
1. Current Electricity (Very High)
2. Electromagnetic Induction (Very High)
3. Ray Optics (Very High)
4. Rotational Motion (Class 11 - Important)

**Chemistry:**
1. Electrochemistry (14 marks)
2. Aldehydes, Ketones, Carboxylic Acids (14 marks)
3. Coordination Compounds
4. Chemical Kinetics

**Mathematics:**
1. Integrals (20 marks - Maximum)
2. Determinants
3. Applications of Derivatives
4. Vectors & 3D Geometry

**Biology:**
1. Molecular Basis of Inheritance
2. Human Reproduction
3. Principles of Inheritance
4. Human Health and Disease
5. Sexual Reproduction in Plants

### Key Exam Features

- **NO Negative Marking**: Attempt all questions
- **80% from Class 12**: Focus heavily on 2nd PUC
- **Experimental Questions**: New addition in 2026
- **State Syllabus**: Follows Karnataka PUC, not just NCERT
- **Speed Important**: 60 questions in 80 minutes (80 seconds/question)

### Study Hours Allocation

- **Total**: 1000 hours (10 months)
- **Physics**: 280 hours
- **Chemistry**: 280 hours
- **Mathematics/Biology**: 280 hours
- **Revision**: 160 hours
