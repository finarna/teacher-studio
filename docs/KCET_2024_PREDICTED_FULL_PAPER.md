### **PART 1: SETS, RELATIONS, FUNCTIONS & TRIGONOMETRY (Q1–Q10)**

**Q1. Topic: Sets (Cardinality)**
**Question:** If $A = \{x : x \text{ is a multiple of 3, } x < 100\}$ and $B = \{x : x \text{ is a multiple of 5, } x < 100\}$, then $n(P(A \cap B))$ is:
(A) $2^6$ (B) $2^7$ (C) $2^{15}$ (D) $2^{20}$
**Correct:** (A)
**Prediction Logic:** KCET 2024 will likely merge Set theory with Power Set definitions. $A \cap B$ are multiples of 15 $\{15, 30, 45, 60, 75, 90\}$. $n=6$.

**Q2. Topic: Relations (Transitivity Trap)**
**Question:** Let $R$ be a relation on $\mathbb{R}$ defined by $aRb \iff 1 + ab > 0$. Then $R$ is:
(A) Reflexive and Symmetric but not Transitive (B) Equivalence (C) Symmetric and Transitive (D) Reflexive only
**Correct:** (A)
**Prediction Logic:** 2023 saw many errors in transitivity. This specific counter-example (e.g., $a=1, b=0.5, c=-2$) is a classic REI "trap" distractor.

**Q3. Topic: Functions (Onto/Into)**
**Question:** $f: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ defined by $f(x) = \frac{x^2}{1+x^2}$. The function is:
(A) One-one and Onto (B) Many-one and Onto (C) Many-one and Into (D) One-one and Into
**Correct:** (C)
**Prediction Logic:** Shift toward range analysis. Since $x^2 \geq 0$, $0 \leq f(x) < 1$. Range $\neq$ Codomain ($\mathbb{R}$), and $f(x)=f(-x)$.

**Q4. Topic: Inverse Trig (Composition)**
**Question:** The value of $\tan(\cos^{-1} \frac{4}{5} + \tan^{-1} \frac{2}{3})$ is:
(A) $6/17$ (B) $17/6$ (C) $7/16$ (D) $16/7$
**Correct:** (B)
**Prediction Logic:** KCET favors conversion identities. $\cos^{-1}(4/5) = \tan^{-1}(3/4)$. Use $\tan(A+B)$.

**Q5. Topic: Trigonometry (General Solution)**
**Question:** If $\sqrt{3}\cos\theta + \sin\theta = \sqrt{2}$, the general value of $\theta$ is:
(A) $n\pi + \frac{\pi}{4} - \frac{\pi}{3}$ (B) $2n\pi \pm \frac{\pi}{4} - \frac{\pi}{6}$ (C) $n\pi + (-1)^n\frac{\pi}{4} - \frac{\pi}{3}$ (D) $2n\pi \pm \frac{\pi}{3} + \frac{\pi}{6}$
**Correct:** (B)
**Prediction Logic:** Synthesis of $a\cos\theta + b\sin\theta$ form.

**Q6-Q10: (Summary)**
