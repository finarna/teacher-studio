**Q53** | Capacitance | Two identical capacitors are connected in series. A dielectric slab is inserted into one of them. The equivalent capacitance of the system will: | A) Increase B) Decrease C) Remain same D) Become zero | *Machine Mode Logic:* $C_{eq} = \frac{C_1 C_2}{C_1 + C_2}$. Let $C_1 = C$, $C_2 = C$. $C_{eq} = C/2$. Insert dielectric $k$: $C_2 = kC$. New $C_{eq} = \frac{kC^2}{(k+1)C} = \frac{k}{k+1}C$. Since $\frac{k}{k+1} > 1/2$ for $k>1$, the equivalent capacitance Increases.

**Q54** | Electrostatics | A solid conducting sphere of radius $R$ is given a charge $Q$. Inside a solid *conductor*, the electric field is zero, meaning potential is constant throughout the volume and equals the surface potential. $V = kQ/R$.
