# AI Marketing & Product Brochure Prompts
*Generated for the EduJourney Universal Teacher Studio — World-Class Designer Aesthetic*

---

## 1. Core Brand Anthem (Hero Image)
**Objective**: Shows the "magic" of AI transforming dry academic content into captivating visuals.

> **Prompt**: A ultra-premium widescreen marketing hero shot. A split-screen composition: on the left, a traditional, dense Science textbook page in monochrome; in the center, a glowing, crystalline AI scan-line is sweeping across. On the right, the page is reborn into a VIBRANT, CAPTIVATING hand-drawn sketchnote. Artistic 3D models of atoms and equations float above the surface. Palette features Electric Teal, Rich Indigo, and Sun-Kissed Amber. Shot in a soft-focus prestigious laboratory setting, high-end commercial photography, 8k resolution, cinematic lighting, looks like a leading Apple-style product launch visual.

## 2. The "Syllabus-Locked" Precision
**Objective**: Markets trust and accuracy, showing that the AI is grounded in official textbooks.

> **Prompt**: A minimalist, high-end graphic design poster. In the center, a perfectly rendered official NCERT/CBSE textbook is encased in a semi-transparent, glowing "Golden Shield" or hexagonal lattice representing the "Syllabus Boundary." Inside the shield, complex formulas and hand-drawn physics diagrams are beautifully organized. The background is a clean, prestigious "Studio Slate" grey. Text overlay style (implied): "Strictly Syllabus, Visually Stunning." Ultra-sharp focus, professional product rendering, studio lighting, highly sophisticated aesthetic.

## 3. Product Features Spread

### A. The "Vault" (Digitization)
*Turning physical paper exams into digital structured data.*
> **Prompt**: A high-action, ultra-sharp product shot. A physical, crumpled exam paper is being fed into a glowing, translucent "Digital Portal." As it passes through, it transforms into a stream of organized, binary data-crystals and sharp, floating UI cards showing question IDs, marks, and analytical metadata. Minimalist laboratory background, "Data Blue" and "Deep Slate" color palette. 8k, ray-tracing, shot on Sony A7R IV style.

### B. The Command Matrix (Heatmap)
*Visualizing curriculum mastery.*
> **Prompt**: A cinematic, widescreen view of a "Digital War Room." In the center, a large, glassmorphic 3D heatmap grid (The Command Matrix) floats in the air. Different cells glow in "Healthy Emerald" (Mastered) or "Warning Amber" (Weakness). A professional 3D-rendered student silhouette is interacting with the grid. Deep Navy and Electric Green accents. Advanced Sci-Fi Analytics style.

### C. Vidya AI (The Universal Teacher)
*The specialized AI tutor.*
> **Prompt**: A conceptual portrait of "Wisdom through Light." A glowing, minimalist abstract entity composed of floating light particles hovering over a tablet. The entity is feeding a "Thread of Knowledge" into the student's device. Background is a dark, prestigious library. Global illumination, soft ethereal glow, masterpiece of digital art.

### D. The Learning Trajectory
*The personalized path to Rank 1.*
> **Prompt**: A majestic landscape shot. A winding, glowing "Path of Success" climbing up a mountain made of floating textbooks and exam papers. Path changes from "Red" at the base to "Emerald" at the peak. Floating milestones mark the way. Cinematic sunset lighting, Unreal Engine 5 render style.
