## 🎨 Design Guidelines

### Color Palette:
- **Primary:** Blue (#3B82F6) - Trust, intelligence
- **Secondary:** Indigo (#6366F1) - Innovation, technology
- **Accent:** Emerald (#10B981) - Growth, success
- **Background:** White/Light gradients

### Typography:
- **Headings:** Outfit (Bold, Black, Uppercase)
- **Body:** Inter or Instrument Sans
- **Numbers/Stats:** Outfit (for impact)

### Visual Style:
- Modern, clean, minimal
- Use screenshots from actual app
- Comparison tables with clear checkmarks/crosses
- Student/teacher photos (diverse, relatable)
- Charts for analytics/performance data

### Tone of Voice:
- **Confident but not arrogant**
- **Data-driven** (use numbers, stats, facts)
- **Student-first** (empathetic, helpful)
- **Professional** for institute materials
- **Avoid hype** (no "revolutionary", "game-changing" overuse)
